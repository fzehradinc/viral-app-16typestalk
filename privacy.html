§ 01

Introduction and Our Privacy Commitment

16TypeTalk ("we," "our," or "us") is built on a simple principle: your thoughts and reflections are yours. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have about your data.

We have written this policy in plain language — not to hide anything, but to be genuinely transparent. We believe you deserve to understand exactly how your data flows through our service.

This Privacy Policy covers the 16TypeTalk mobile application and any related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

Privacy Contact

Privacy & data requests: 16typestalk@proton.me

Legal & terms inquiries: 16typestalk_app@proton.me

Website: 16typetalk.app

§ 02

About 16TypeTalk

16TypeTalk is an AI-powered personal reflection application. The Service presents multiple MBTI-inspired personality perspectives ("the Council") in response to situations, decisions, or questions you share. Our goal is to help you think more clearly by offering diverse, structured viewpoints — not to replace human connection, professional advice, or therapy.

MBTI® and Myers-Briggs Type Indicator® are registered trademarks of The Myers & Briggs Foundation. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by The Myers & Briggs Foundation. Our use of MBTI-inspired concepts is for self-reflection and entertainment purposes only.

§ 03

Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use the Service, you may provide the following categories of information:

Authentication Data

  • Sign in with Apple: We receive a unique user identifier and, at your option, your name and/or email address. We never receive or store your Apple ID password.
  • Google Sign-In: We receive your Google account identifier, display name, and email address. We never receive or store your Google password.
  • You may also use limited features as a guest without creating an account.

Content You Submit

  • Text prompts, questions, and situations you share with the Council
  • Your voting choices (which character perspective resonated most)
  • Follow-up messages and reactions during discussions
  • User preferences (language selection, notification settings)
Important: We strongly recommend you do not include highly sensitive personal information in your prompts, such as full financial account numbers, complete medical records, or detailed legal case information identifying third parties.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version number
  • Crash reports and error logs (to identify and fix technical issues)
  • Basic usage analytics: which screens you visit, features you use, and session duration — not used for advertising
  • App performance data for service improvement

3.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise geolocation data
  • Contacts, photos, or other files without explicit permission
  • Biometric data of any kind
  • Cross-app or cross-website tracking data
  • Advertising profiles
  • Your personal information for sale to third parties — ever
§ 04

How We Use Your Information

4.1 Core Service Delivery

  • Processing your text prompts through AI models to generate Council perspectives
  • Storing your session history and reports so you can review them
  • Personalizing your experience based on your preferences
  • Tracking your usage streak and session statistics

4.2 Service Improvement and Safety

  • Diagnosing and fixing technical bugs using crash reports
  • Understanding which features are most useful through anonymized analytics
  • Maintaining security and detecting abuse

4.3 Communications

  • Service-related notifications (if enabled)
  • Responding to privacy inquiries and support requests
  • Notifying you of material changes to this Policy

We do not use your personal data or conversation content for advertising or marketing profiling.

§ 05

AI Processing & Third-Party Disclosure

Apple App Store Compliance (Guideline 5.1.2(i)): We explicitly disclose that your prompt text is shared with third-party AI services. Your explicit consent is obtained before any data is transmitted.

5.1 How AI Processing Works

When you submit a prompt to the Council, the text of your message is transmitted to external AI service providers to generate responses. This is the core technical mechanism of the Service.

5.2 Current AI Providers

  • Anthropic, Inc. (Claude) — for generating personality-based responses

These providers act as data processors on our behalf. They are contractually prohibited from using your prompts to train their models without separate disclosure and consent.

5.3 Your Explicit Consent

By submitting a prompt to the Council, you explicitly consent to your prompt text being transmitted to our AI service providers. This consent is displayed clearly during onboarding. You may withdraw consent at any time by ceasing to submit new prompts.

5.4 What Is NOT Sent to AI Providers

  • Your name or email address
  • Your Apple or Google account identifiers
  • Your device information
  • Your usage analytics or behavioral data
§ 06

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these specific circumstances:

6.1 Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting: For storing session data and reports
  • AI API: Anthropic (Claude) — see Section 5
  • Authentication: Apple, Google
  • Diagnostics: Crash reporting for technical issues
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics

6.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by applicable law, regulation, or valid legal process. Where permitted by law, we will notify you before disclosing.

6.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger or acquisition, you will be notified at least 30 days in advance via email or in-app notice.

§ 07

Data Retention

  • Account & profile data: Retained while account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Session history & reports: Retained until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Crash logs: Deleted after 90 days.
  • Analytics: Anonymized after 12 months.
  • Backups: Cleared within 90 days of original deletion.
§ 08

Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • Open 16TypeTalk → Profile tab
  • Tap Settings → Delete Account
  • Confirm identity and confirm deletion

All personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Backups are cleared within 90 days. This is irreversible.

Can't delete in-app?

Email 16typestalk@proton.me with subject "Account Deletion Request" and the email associated with your account.

§ 09

Your Privacy Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion (see Section 8)
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Request we limit processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for AI processing at any time
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests

Email 16typestalk@proton.me with subject "Privacy Rights Request." We respond within 30 days. EEA/UK residents may also contact their local data protection authority (edpb.europa.eu).

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to know categories and specific pieces of data collected
  • Right to know purposes for each category
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information — we do not sell data
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights
§ 10

Children's Privacy

16TypeTalk is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided data, contact 16typestalk@proton.me immediately with subject "Child Data Request."

Users aged 13–17 should use the Service only with parental consent and supervision.

§ 11

Emotional and Mental Health Safety

⚠️ If you are experiencing a mental health crisis or emergency, please contact emergency services immediately. Do not use 16TypeTalk in emergencies.
  • 16TypeTalk is NOT a mental health treatment or crisis intervention service
  • AI responses are automatically generated and may not be appropriate for all situations
  • We encourage you to use AI-generated content as one perspective among many
  • Please seek human support when needed
§ 12

Data Security

  • All data in transit encrypted via TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Stored data encrypted with industry-standard encryption
  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • All staff with data access bound by confidentiality agreements

In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by law, without undue delay.

§ 13

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries. When we transfer data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland to countries without an adequacy decision, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other valid transfer mechanisms. Contact 16typestalk@proton.me to request information about transfer mechanisms.

§ 14

The 16TypeTalk mobile application does not use tracking cookies. We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA or GAID) for advertising purposes. Only the minimum technical identifiers necessary to maintain your session and account are used.
