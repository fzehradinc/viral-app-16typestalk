ENTJ
The Commander
Cuts through sentiment. Demands clarity. Will ask the question you've been avoiding.
"Stop waiting for perfect conditions. What's the cost of another six months of inaction?"
🔭
INTJ
The Architect
Sees the system behind the problem. Maps what you're not accounting for.
"You're solving the symptom. What's the actual pattern that keeps producing this?"
💡
ENTP
The Debater
Challenges every assumption. Proposes the option you dismissed too quickly.
"What if the thing you think is the problem is actually the solution in disguise?"
🧮
INTP
The Logician
Builds the logic map. Finds the flaw in your reasoning you didn't notice.
"Walk me through the assumption underneath that. Is it actually true?"
🌙
INFJ
The Advocate
Senses what you're not saying. Points to the deeper pattern behind your words.
"You said 'fine' three times. What's the thing you're not letting yourself want?"
🌊
INFP
The Mediator
Grounds every decision in values. Asks if the path aligns with who you really are.
"Success means nothing if you're becoming someone you don't recognize."
🔥
ENFJ
The Protagonist
Sees your potential first. Pushes you toward the version of yourself you're capable of being.
"What would you do if you were already the person you're trying to become?"
🌈
ENFP
The Campaigner
Finds the spark in the impossible. Reminds you what you almost forgot you wanted.
"The boring option is always available. What's the version that actually excites you?"
🛡
ISFJ
The Defender
Thinks about the people you'll affect. The quiet guardian of what matters most.
"Who else is carrying the weight of this decision with you, even if they don't know it?"
📋
ISTJ
The Logistician
The reality check. Asks what the plan actually looks like on Monday morning.
"What's the first concrete step, and what's the first obstacle after it?"
🤝
ESFJ
The Consul
Maps the relational landscape. Who will be affected, and how will they receive this?
"How does this change your relationship with the people closest to you?"
📣
ESTJ
The Executive
Demands accountability. Cuts to the timeline and the result — no ambiguity.
"Decide. Then execute. Revisiting the decision every week is the decision."
🎨
ISFP
The Artist
Asks if this choice lets you live authentically, or just comfortably.
"Is this path beautiful? Not aesthetically — as a life?"
🔧
ISTP
The Virtuoso
Solves the practical angle. What's the simplest version of this that actually works?
"Remove everything that's not essential. What's left?"
🎭
ESFP
The Entertainer
Asks what you'd do if fear wasn't part of the equation. Then makes you answer honestly.
"If a year from now this works, what does that day feel like?"

Calculates the real risk vs. the perceived risk. Then challenges you to act on the data.
